CNA Class

Theory studies and clinical preparation for State nursing assistant certifcation.

This last and highly used version of my online CNA training, which existed from December 2006 thru May 2017, was the first private, independently-operated CNA program in Montana. It brought me goal-oriented students from across MT to finish the course with their "hand-on" clinical skills & MT certification exam in my lab and in my assisted living facility. The class utilized other sites, including: Youtube, Google Docs, Scribd, StudyStack and KeepAndShare. The site ranked high in google searches, and all that was required for the class was accessible from this page.

  • Date: August 2013-May 2017
  • Client: Heart and Hands CNA Training
  • Category: Educational Course
